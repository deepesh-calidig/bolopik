How to make predictions and get points?

  1. Register to www.bolopik.com
  2. When games are available, you will receive a notification by email
  3. Visit our website and select the games you want to predict the results (make sure you enter your predictions 15 minutes before the game starts)
Each sport has its rules to get points:
  1. Soccer: enter the final score of the game
  2. Basketball: enter the number of quarters each team will win
  3. Tennis: enter the number of sets each player will win
