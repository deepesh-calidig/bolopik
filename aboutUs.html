VISION

Our Vision in BoloPik is to be a leading website worldwide with the most diverse platforms for sports predictions, reward program.

BoloPik is a website dedicated to sport where users can predict their results and be rewarded for their wins. Our Reward Program is available in different countries, and we keep working on its expansion to as many BoloPikers as possible.

At BoloPik, users accumulate points, and in the future (not yet available) we will have the option to convert them in coupons that can be redeemed at sport stores, hotels, restaurants, gas station, etc.





What makes BoloPik different ?

BoloPik offers a variety of options to the users that define us as an “all-in-one” website:



"Everything for FREE: News, Predictions and Fantasy Games"
Reward program
Coupon redeemable in different entities




How to make predictions and get points?

It’s easy! You just need to follow these 3 steps to make predictions and get points:

  1. Register to www.bolopik.com
  2. When games are available, you will receive a notification by email
  3. Visit our website and select the games you want to predict the results (make sure you enter your predictions 15 minutes before the game starts)

Each sport has its rules to get points:

  1. Soccer: enter the final score of the game
  2. Basketball: enter the number of quarters each team will win
  3. Tennis: enter the number of sets each player will win
